## Risk Factors & Mitigation Strategies

### Risk 1: Carrier Appointment Losses
**Risk**: Insurance carrier exits WV market or terminates agency appointment
**Mitigation**: 
- Maintain 8-10 carrier relationships (diversification)
- Monitor carrier financial strength ratings
- Build strong production with multiple carriers
- Stay compliant with all carrier requirements

### Risk 2: Team Capacity Constraints
**Risk**: Growth outpaces team's ability to service clients
**Mitigation**:
- Hire CSR by mid-2025 (before critical capacity)
- Implement AMS automation by 2026
- Monitor service quality metrics monthly
- Cap new client acquisition if service suffers

### Risk 3: Economic Downturn in West Virginia
**Risk**: Job losses, population decline impact insurance purchasing
**Mitigation**:
- Focus on essential coverage (auto, home - required by lenders)
- Competitive pricing through multi-carrier shopping
- Payment plan flexibility
- Commercial lines growth (more stable)

### Risk 4: Technology Implementation Failures
**Risk**: AMS implementation disrupts operations or fails to deliver ROI
**Mitigation**:
- Thorough vendor selection process with references
- Phased implementation (not "big bang" approach)
- Extensive staff training before go-live
- Maintain backup systems during transition

### Risk 5: Competitive Pressure
**Risk**: National online insurers or larger agencies increase marketing
**Mitigation**:
- Double down on local presence and personal service
- Claims advocacy as key differentiator
- Community involvement and visibility
- Superior Google reviews and online reputation
