## About Abel Insurance Group

Abel Insurance Group is a **local, family-owned independent insurance agency** that has been serving Buckhannon and Upshur County for several years. Led by Joyce and Travis Abel, the agency provides:

- **Personal Lines Insurance**: Home, Auto, Umbrella, Watercraft
- **Specialty Insurance**: High-value homes, recreational vehicles, unique properties
- **Growing Commercial Lines**: Small business, contractors, professional liability
- **Claims Advocacy**: Hands-on support during disasters (proven during 2023 Upshur County floods)

### What Makes Us Different

1. **Truly Local**: Physical office in Buckhannon - walk in and talk to the same family every time
2. **True Choice**: Represent 8-10 insurance carriers (not captive to one company like State Farm or Allstate)
3. **Family-Owned Service**: Treat clients like neighbors, not policy numbers
4. **Claims Advocacy**: Personal support when disasters strike
5. **West Virginia Expertise**: Deep understanding of local risks (floods, harsh winters, rural properties)

### Current Stats
- **Clients**: 250-300 active households
- **Service Area**: Buckhannon, Tennerton, French Creek, Rock Cave, and surrounding Upshur County
- **Team**: Joyce Abel, Travis Abel, and part-time customer service representative
- **Carriers**: 8-10 personal lines carriers, growing commercial portfolio
